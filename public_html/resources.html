Go Resources on the Web


Sensei's Library - Sensei's is a collaborative Go site, where everyone can contribute. It contains a large quantity of Go information.

GoProblems.com - This site is home to many java-based Go problems, the problems can be sorted by genre or difficulty.

Go Base - Go Base is a database of go information, with a large collection of professional games, fuseki, and joseki.

AGA - The American Go Association is the coordinating body for the go clubs of the United States. See their many web resources.
